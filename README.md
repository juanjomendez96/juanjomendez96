## About Me

I'm a Data Engineer with **6+ years of experience** designing and operating resilient data pipelines — from raw ingestion to orchestration, observability, and governance. I bridge rigorous data engineering with modern AI tooling, building Python-first workflows in Databricks for regulated clinical environments.

I'm driven by a simple belief: **good data infrastructure is invisible** — and great data products change how people make decisions. In healthcare and clinical research, that matters deeply.

Beyond the day job, I'm a lifelong learner always exploring what's next — whether that's applying LLMs to data platform workflows, building side projects to sharpen new skills, or picking up a new framework just to see what it can do.
